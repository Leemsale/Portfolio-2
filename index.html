About

My journey into tech began with a childhood fascination for technology, but I was clueless about where to start. After exploring various paths and completing my education, I finally discovered my interest in web development and enrolled in a Web Development Bootcamp. Since then, I’ve built a solid foundation in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, Node.js, and PostgreSQL.
Ever since my very first HTML project, my excitement and curiosity have only intensified. I specialize in creating dynamic, responsive websites that not only look great but also deliver a seamless user experience across devices. Whether it’s crafting an engaging frontend or developing a strong backend, I’m dedicated to delivering high-quality solutions.
I’m excited to add value with the skills I’ve gained and to make a positive impact on users. Let’s connect and bring your ideas to life!
